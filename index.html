Meir Yossef Levi

I received my BSc and MSc from the Technion Institute of Technology in Haifa, Israel. I am at the final stages of my PHd under the guidance of Prof. Guy Gilboa from the Electrical & Computer Engineering faculty.

My research focuses on gaining a deep understanding of the inner mechanisms of machine learning systems, with a particular emphasis on vision-language models. I study the geometry and probabilistic structure of their latent spaces. Previously, my work centered on 3D computer vision, especially robust point cloud classification.
